Articles & blogs 

Graphics

http://www.songho.ca/opengl/gl_projectionmatrix.html - Article on how to generate a projection matrix

https://www.scratchapixel.com/ - Scratchapixel is very thorough (possibly gives you a lot of redundant information) and is focused more on ray tracing than rasterising.

https://github.com/ssloy/tinyrenderer/wiki/Lesson-0:-getting-started - Tinyrenderer. If you're looking to program something & get a feel for how graphics APIs like DirectX and OpenGL might work under the hood, take a look at this. 

http://www.realtimerendering.com/raytracing/Ray%20Tracing%20in%20a%20Weekend.pdf - If you want to get into raytracing, this is a good place to start.

http://immersivemath.com/ and https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLZHQObOWTQDPD3MizzM2xVFitgF8hE_ab - If you're going to do 3D graphics and want to take a look at the math involved, take a look here
https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PL_w_qWAQZtAZhtzPI5pkAtcUVgmzdAP8g - General and mostly math and theory focussed graphics course.

https://www.reddit.com/r/GraphicsProgramming/comments/bbny2s/recommendations_for_computer_graphics_courses/ - r/GraphicsProgramming thread with some other recommendations

https://media.contentapi.ea.com/content/dam/eacom/frostbite/files/s2016-pbs-frostbite-sky-clouds-new.pdf - Paper on real-time sky rendering
